Endpoint management is one of the most critical — and most contested — areas of enterprise governance. Every organization depends on endpoints, yet many still struggle to answer a fundamental question: who actually owns these devices? In many environments, IT and security teams are both confident they’re doing the right thing, yet still talk past each other.
