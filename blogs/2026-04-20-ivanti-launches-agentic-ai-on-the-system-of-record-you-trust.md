<p>Investors and enterprises&nbsp;are finally asking&nbsp;the question&nbsp;they'd&nbsp;been avoiding: which software companies will survive the AI revolution, and which will be made obsolete by it? The answer is becoming clear. Companies that serve as the system of record, the authoritative source of truth that AI itself depends on,&nbsp;are essential.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Today, Ivanti is&nbsp;announcing&nbsp;a controlled release of the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ivanti.com/ai/agenticai">Ivanti Neurons&nbsp;AI Self-Service Agent</a>,&nbsp;our first autonomous AI solution.&nbsp;We're&nbsp;building&nbsp;from a position of strategic strength,&nbsp;introducing&nbsp;the new solution&nbsp;initially within our&nbsp;IT Service Management (ITSM)&nbsp;framework, building on our long history of intelligent automation&nbsp;through&nbsp;built-in workflows, our Neurons bot infrastructure, generative AI tools&nbsp;and now a fully conversational autonomous agent.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Building the foundation to scale AI&nbsp;</h2>

<p>There's&nbsp;no shortage of&nbsp;talk&nbsp;about what AI will eventually do,&nbsp;but that potential means nothing&nbsp;without&nbsp;a strong&nbsp;foundation&nbsp;beneath it.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>Operationalizing autonomous AI and scaling its impact across an entire organization requires five foundational capabilities.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<ol>
	<li><strong>Knowing what exists:&nbsp;</strong>AI must&nbsp;operate&nbsp;on&nbsp;accurate&nbsp;discovery data, not assumptions. Without real-time visibility into devices, users, configurations&nbsp;and dependencies, autonomous actions become dangerous.&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Maintaining&nbsp;institutional memory:&nbsp;</strong>AI needs&nbsp;durable&nbsp;context that survives organizational change. Relationships,&nbsp;history&nbsp;and dependencies&nbsp;must be preserved in a system of record.&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Owning accountability:&nbsp;</strong>Every autonomous action needs clear ownership and a decision&nbsp;trail. When AI acts on behalf of the organization, someone must be accountable.&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Enforcing policy:&nbsp;</strong>AI must distinguish between&nbsp;what's&nbsp;technically possible and&nbsp;what's&nbsp;organizationally permissible. Optimization without governance creates compliance risk.&nbsp;</li>
	<li><strong>Ensuring auditability:&nbsp;</strong>Every action or decision made&nbsp;through AI&nbsp;must be traceable,&nbsp;explainable&nbsp;and defensible in an audit.&nbsp;</li>
</ol>

<p>That's&nbsp;not a limitation of any&nbsp;model,&nbsp;but rather the nature of how AI works. AI is powerful, but it&nbsp;operates&nbsp;on data. And if that data is fragmented, inaccurate&nbsp;or ungoverned, the AI built on top of it will be too.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This is&nbsp;what&nbsp;the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ivanti.com/ivanti-neurons">Ivanti Neurons Platform</a>&nbsp;was built to provide. Our Neurons Platform serves&nbsp;as&nbsp;a robust&nbsp;system of record and control&nbsp;panel&nbsp;for IT and security operations. Our Discovery Engine&nbsp;establishes&nbsp;ground truth.&nbsp;Our&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ivanti.com/glossary/cmdb">CMDB</a>&nbsp;preserves relationships, dependencies, and change history. Our&nbsp;<a href="https://www.ivanti.com/products/it-asset-management">IT Asset Management&nbsp;(ITAM)</a>&nbsp;capabilities assign ownership, lifecycle, and accountability. Our Software Estate Management enforces&nbsp;what's&nbsp;allowed versus&nbsp;what's&nbsp;merely detected.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>This&nbsp;is&nbsp;more than&nbsp;product&nbsp;architecture.&nbsp;It's&nbsp;the foundation&nbsp;your organization needs&nbsp;to&nbsp;operate&nbsp;securely and intelligently.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>The&nbsp;journey to&nbsp;autonomous&nbsp;service&nbsp;delivery&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Agentic&nbsp;AI&nbsp;wasn’t&nbsp;built overnight.&nbsp;At Ivanti,&nbsp;we’ve&nbsp;been building toward this deliberately&nbsp;with a consistent focus on trust, governance,&nbsp;and repeatability.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Our path to autonomous service delivery was strategic and intentional.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Traditional automation:&nbsp;Established&nbsp;rule-based workflows executing predefined tasks in sequence.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>Cognitive AI:&nbsp;Added intelligence&nbsp;through bots, machine&nbsp;learning&nbsp;and predictive analytics, moving IT from reactive to proactive.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Generative AI:&nbsp;Introduced&nbsp;large language models and natural language interaction.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Conversational AI:&nbsp;Deepened interactions by adding intent recognition, sentiment detection, and safety guardrails transforming AI from a tool into an interactive partner.&nbsp;</p>

<p>And now,&nbsp;autonomous agents&nbsp;that&nbsp;don't&nbsp;just respond but orchestrate actions across systems while&nbsp;maintaining&nbsp;governance at every step.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Each stage&nbsp;was&nbsp;built on the&nbsp;prior one.&nbsp;And each stage delivered real enterprise value&nbsp;only&nbsp;because it was grounded in&nbsp;accurate&nbsp;data,&nbsp;governance&nbsp;and accountability. That foundation is what makes today's announcement possible.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Introducing&nbsp;Ivanti Neurons AI Self-Service Agent&nbsp;</h2>

<p>The problem is one every IT leader knows well&nbsp;—&nbsp;employees struggle to find answers scattered across disconnected knowledge systems. Basic tickets flood the service desk. Users abandon confusing portals in frustration.&nbsp;IT teams get trapped on a ticket treadmill, doing repetitive work instead of the strategic projects that actually move the business forward.&nbsp;Traditional self-service portals&nbsp;haven't&nbsp;solved this.&nbsp;They're&nbsp;rigid, frustrating, and often create more problems than they resolve.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Ivanti&nbsp;Neurons AI Self-Service Agent&nbsp;is different.&nbsp;We’ve&nbsp;all seen&nbsp;chatbots, but this is something else.&nbsp;It’s&nbsp;a true conversational&nbsp;AI agent.&nbsp;It converses, investigates,&nbsp;resolves&nbsp;and escalates only when it needs to. The first release focuses on intelligent knowledge&nbsp;search, incident&nbsp;escalation&nbsp;and&nbsp;the ability to&nbsp;request something from IT using natural language.&nbsp;It feels as easy as texting a friend.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This&nbsp;initial&nbsp;release&nbsp;delivers&nbsp;three&nbsp;things done exceptionally well: intelligent knowledge&nbsp;search, incident&nbsp;escalation when knowledge&nbsp;isn't&nbsp;enough,&nbsp;and the ability to&nbsp;request from&nbsp;a service catalog without the complexity of self-service portal&nbsp;forms.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>We understand&nbsp;that time, speed,&nbsp;and accuracy are non-negotiable in the digital era&nbsp;and have&nbsp;built&nbsp;the capabilities with that in mind.&nbsp;The AI&nbsp;Self-Service Agent&nbsp;engages in natural conversation, asks the right questions, queries across internal and approved external sources,&nbsp;and surfaces verified answers. If this process alone&nbsp;doesn't&nbsp;resolve the issue,&nbsp;the agent&nbsp;escalates and captures a structured incident from the conversation&nbsp;without&nbsp;requiring the user to&nbsp;repeat their&nbsp;request, ensuring a frictionless user experience.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This solution is&nbsp;built on an AI framework designed to grow with it&nbsp;as we execute our full vision for autonomous endpoint management.&nbsp;</p>
