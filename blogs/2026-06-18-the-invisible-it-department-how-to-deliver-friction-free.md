Examines why most AI initiatives fail to deliver measurable ROI and how organizations can build successful agentic AI deployments by prioritizing user experience over technological capability. Outlines a deployment framework: identify pain points before capabilities, deploy AI as infrastructure rather than interface, measure user outcomes instead of AI performance metrics, and establish governance that expands as systems prove reliable. Real-world examples show agentic AI compressing resolution times from days to seconds and shifting IT teams from reactive firefighting to strategic initiatives.
