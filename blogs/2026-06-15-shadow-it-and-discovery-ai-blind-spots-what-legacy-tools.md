Addresses visibility gaps in modern IT environments where legacy discovery tools fail to keep pace with cloud velocity, SaaS proliferation, and AI service adoption. Identifies five critical limitations: fragmented visibility across multiple tools, agent-based discovery that misses unmanaged assets, security blind spots from unseen resources, slow point-in-time scanning, and manual reconciliation across disconnected data sources. Proposes a platform-governed visibility model using continuous discovery and unified intelligence layers that normalize data across hybrid environments to enable autonomous endpoint management and exposure remediation.
