Continuing the Patch Apocalypse this month we are already seeing some more aggressive shifts in updates from many vendors. Oracle announced a new release cadence starting in May 2026 to address the acceleration of vulnerability detection introduced by Mythos and other AI security models. Monthly Critical Security Patch Update (CSPUs) will fill in the two-month gap between their quarterly Critical Patch Update (CPU).
